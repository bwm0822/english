Unit 01 Exercises

Fill-in-the-Blank Questions

  1. 1. I went _______ yesterday. (我昨天去滑雪橇)
    • (a) sledding
    • (b) skating
    • (c) swimming
  2. 2. He is practicing _______. (他在練習空手道)
    • (a) skating
    • (b) swimming
    • (c) karate
  3. 3. We went to the _______ last weekend. (我們上週末去了海灘)
    • (a) park
    • (b) beach
    • (c) zoo
  4. 4. She wore a _______ to the pool. (她穿著泳衣去游泳池)
    • (a) hat
    • (b) swimsuit
    • (c) shorts
  5. 5. We saw _______ in the sky. (我們看到了閃電)
    • (a) rain
    • (b) thunder
    • (c) lightning
  6. 6. He is a _______. (他是一名動物園管理員)
    • (a) student
    • (b) teacher
    • (c) zookeeper
  7. 7. They went _______ last winter. (他們去年冬天去滑雪)
    • (a) swimming
    • (b) skiing
    • (c) skating
  8. 8. We had a _______ yesterday. (我們昨天有一個練習)
    • (a) study
    • (b) practice
    • (c) swing
  9. 9. He wore _______ to the beach. (他穿著短褲去海灘)
    • (a) pants
    • (b) shorts
    • (c) swimsuit
  10. 10. It was my _______ day. (那是我最喜歡的一天)
    • (a) worst
    • (b) longest
    • (c) favorite

Reading Comprehension

Yesterday, I went to the beach with my family. We saw a zookeeper with a hat. We played in the sand and swam in the ocean. It was my favorite day.

  1. 1. Where did I go yesterday?
    • (a) To the zoo
    • (b) To the park
    • (c) To the beach
  2. 2. Who did I see at the beach?
    • (a) A teacher
    • (b) A zookeeper
    • (c) A student
  3. 3. What did we do in the sand?
    • (a) Studied
    • (b) Practiced
    • (c) Played
  4. 4. Where did we swim?
    • (a) In the pool
    • (b) In the lake
    • (c) In the ocean
  5. 5. What was my favorite day?
    • (a) Today
    • (b) Tomorrow
    • (c) Yesterday
