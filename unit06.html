Reading Comprehension

There are many books in the library. These books are very interesting. Those books are about history. The library is near the park. It is my favorite place to read.

  1. 1. Where are the books?
    • (a) In the store
    • (b) In the library
    • (c) In the park
  2. 2. What are the books about?
    • (a) Science
    • (b) History
    • (c) Math
  3. 3. Where is the library?
    • (a) Near the store
    • (b) Near the park
    • (c) Near the school
  4. 4. What is my favorite place to read?
    • (a) The store
    • (b) The park
    • (c) The library
