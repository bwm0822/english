Reading Comprehension

Yesterday, we went to the amusement park. It was a breezy day, perfect for outdoor activities. We saw a circus performance and had lunch at a restaurant. Later, we visited the theater to watch a movie. It was a fun-filled day.

  1. 1. Where did we go yesterday?
    • (a) To the hospital
    • (b) To the amusement park
    • (c) To the library
  2. 2. What was the weather like?
    • (a) Stormy
    • (b) Warm
    • (c) Breezy
  3. 3. What did we see at the amusement park?
    • (a) A movie
    • (b) A circus performance
    • (c) A library
  4. 4. Where did we have lunch?
    • (a) At a restaurant
    • (b) At the hospital
    • (c) At the airport
  5. 5. What did we do after lunch?
    • (a) Went shopping
    • (b) Visited the theater
    • (c) Went to the library
