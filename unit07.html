Reading Comprehension

Yesterday, I cleaned the house. I started in the kitchen and then moved to the bathroom. After that, I cleaned the bedroom and the dining room. Finally, I organized the garage. It was a lot of work, but now the house is very clean.

  1. 1. Where did I start cleaning?
    • (a) In the bathroom
    • (b) In the kitchen
    • (c) In the bedroom
  2. 2. Where did I clean after the kitchen?
    • (a) The bathroom
    • (b) The garage
    • (c) The dining room
  3. 3. What did I do last?
    • (a) Cleaned the bedroom
    • (b) Organized the garage
    • (c) Cleaned the dining room
  4. 4. How is the house now?
    • (a) Dirty
    • (b) Clean
    • (c) Messy
