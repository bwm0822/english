Reading Comprehension

Last weekend, we went to the park. We saw a sloth hanging from a tree branch. The children played with their pets and ate popcorn. It was a fun day.

  1. 1. Where did we go last weekend?
    • (a) To the zoo
    • (b) To the park
    • (c) To the beach
  2. 2. What did we see hanging from a tree branch?
    • (a) A sloth
    • (b) A bird
    • (c) A snail
  3. 3. What did the children play with?
    • (a) Toys
    • (b) Pets
    • (c) Snowflakes
  4. 4. What did we eat?
    • (a) Popcorn
    • (b) Snowflakes
    • (c) Branches
  5. 5. How was the day?
    • (a) Boring
    • (b) Fun
    • (c) Long
