Reading Comprehension

Spring is a beautiful season. The weather is warm and flowers bloom. In summer, we go to the beach and enjoy the sun. Fall is cool and the leaves change color. Winter is cold and we play in the snow.

  1. 1. What is the weather like in spring?
    • (a) Cold
    • (b) Warm
    • (c) Hot
  2. 2. Where do we go in summer?
    • (a) To the mountains
    • (b) To the beach
    • (c) To the city
  3. 3. What happens to the leaves in fall?
    • (a) They bloom
    • (b) They fall
    • (c) They change color
  4. 4. What do we do in winter?
    • (a) Swim
    • (b) Play in the snow
    • (c) Go to the beach
  5. 5. How many seasons are there in a year?
    • (a) Three
    • (b) Four
    • (c) Five
