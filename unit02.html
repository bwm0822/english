Reading Comprehension

Yesterday, the weather was warm and breezy. We went to the park and saw a great big rock. We played under the tree and enjoyed the day. It was a wonderful day.

  1. What was the weather like yesterday?
    • (a) Cold and rainy
    • (b) Hot and sunny
    • (c) Warm and breezy
  2. Where did we go?
    • (a) To the zoo
    • (b) To the park
    • (c) To the beach
  3. What did we see in the park?
    • (a) A big cloud
    • (b) A great big rock
    • (c) A small tree
  4. What did we do under the tree?
    • (a) Studied
    • (b) Practiced
    • (c) Played
  5. How was the day?
    • (a) Terrible
    • (b) Boring
    • (c) Wonderful
